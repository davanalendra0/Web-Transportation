Our Story

Born from a passion for safety and an eye for beauty — Bronze Sparks exists to redefine what premium riding means.

A brand rooted in purpose

Bronze Sparks was founded on a single belief: that safety should never come at the cost of style. Our founders — both riders and dreamers — set out to create scooters that couples could truly fall in love with.

From our first prototype in a small Jakarta workshop to a nationally recognized name, every step has been guided by care, craftsmanship, and an unwavering commitment to the people who ride with us.

We believe that a scooter is more than a vehicle. It is a vessel for shared experiences — first rides, spontaneous detours, and quiet evenings together.
